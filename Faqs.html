FAQs

1. Is permanent makeup a safe procedure to have done by a professional, and what are the potential risks involved?

+

Yes, permanent makeup is safe when performed by a trained and certified professional using sterile equipment and high-quality pigments. Potential risks are minimal and can include slight redness or swelling, which are temporary.

2. What's the difference between lip blush and lip neutralization?

+

Lip blush enhances the natural color and shape of your lips, giving a soft tint for a youthful look. Lip neutralization is designed for clients with darker or uneven lip tones and works to balance and brighten the natural lip color before adding a desired shade.

3. What's the difference between microblading and nano brows?

+

Microblading uses a manual tool to create hair-like strokes, while nano brows use a machine with a fine needle for more precise and longer-lasting results. Nano brows tend to be gentler on the skin and are better for oily or sensitive skin types.
